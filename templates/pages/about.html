Our Story

The People Behind
the Pieces

Claws & Crystals began as a small workshop in Pune, driven by one belief: that Indian jewellery deserves a global stage. We work directly with artisans across Rajasthan, Tamil Nadu, and West Bengal to bring their generations-old techniques to you — unfiltered and unapologetic.

Our Values

Craft First, Always

We believe in paying artisans fairly, sourcing materials responsibly, and designing pieces that outlast trends. Every item in our collection tells the story of the hands that made it.

We're a small team, and we like it that way — it means every order gets personal attention, and every customer relationship matters.
